About

Engineer who
thinks in data

I completed a dual degree in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Bhubaneswar, one of India's premier technical institutions. Post-graduation, I spent three years as an HVAC Engineer at AECOM — a global infrastructure firm — where I designed and analysed complex building systems.

That hands-on engineering experience gave me a deep appreciation for how real-world systems generate data, and how that data — when properly analysed — can drive smarter decisions. This conviction led me to pursue a PGDBA from the prestigious tri-institutional program spanning ISI Kolkata, IIT Kharagpur, and IIM Calcutta.

Based in Howrah, West Bengal, I'm passionate about applying machine learning, statistical modelling, and data engineering to solve meaningful engineering and business problems.
